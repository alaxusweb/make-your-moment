PDF EDITING

The artwork, title, frames, bonuses, and concept seal are raster artwork.
Only the day-sticker name labels are editable PDF text. Your PDF editor may
replace the embedded label font with another Japanese font installed on your
computer. Long replacement text may need a smaller font size.

PRINTING

For the intended physical size, choose Actual Size or 100%. Fit to Page may
reduce the artwork. A transparent PDF page can appear white in some viewers.
